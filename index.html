Alfam

        
Al Faham Chicken is a type of grilled chicken in the Arabian Gulf region. The dish is quite popular in many parts of India, especially Kerala. The spices used in AlFaham chicken is different from the normal grilled chicken.


        

        
    

    

        

        

        
        
Pizza

        
Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.


        

        
    

    

        

        

        
        
Sadya

        
Sadya is a platter of Kerala origin and of importance to all Malayalis, consisting of a variety of traditional vegetarian platters usually served on a banana leaf in Kerala as lunch. Sadya is typically served as a traditional platter for Onam, the state festival of Kerala, Vishu.
